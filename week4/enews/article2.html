Quantum Computing Breakthrough in Silicon Valley

By Michael Chen | Published: February 10, 2025 | Category: Technology

On February 10, 2025, QuantumTech Inc. in Silicon Valley unveiled a quantum computer capable of performing 1 trillion calculations per second, outstripping classical computers by orders of magnitude. This breakthrough promises to transform cryptography, AI, and scientific research. The new system uses advanced quantum bits (qubits) stabilized at near-absolute zero, opening new frontiers in computational power.
